ABOUT ME

I'm Fredrick and I love Technology

An Immigrant, An Extrovert and a Software Engineer.
An Immigrant of Sierra-Leone West Africa, I immigrated to the us in 2014 to learn and gain valuable life lessons and skills. I studied Computer Science at the College of Dupage in chicago Illinois. Relocation to Charlotte Nc preempted me from graduating. I've always had intense curiosity for technology and i always wanted to indulged in that field of study. Road To Hire was a dream come true for me as i'm sure it is for others, It's a 6 months rigorous course in which one gets to study and practice multiple coding languages such as Html, CSS and Javascipt. During my time in Road to Hire, I've been able to work with teams and independently to build projects with the help of Javascript libraries and frameworks like Node.js, Mysql and React.js such as an E-commerce website that sells video games and consoles, A website that gives and updates information to the public about human trafficking and highlights areas that a of high risks. We also built a website using 3rd-party API's to inform the public about air pollution and had an active functionality that'll tell the exact location and air quality of the current location at any giving time. Several other websites and applications were build by me and my team, please check out my projects page.
